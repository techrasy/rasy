What is ERP?

YOUR ENTIRE
BUSINESS IN
ONE PLATFORM

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) is a category of business management software that integrates all core business processes — finance, supply chain, operations, HR, and customer management — into a single, real-time system of record.

Instead of siloed spreadsheets and disconnected apps, every department works from the same live data. Decisions become faster, errors disappear, and growth no longer breaks your operations.

🔍

Discovery & System Design

Phases 01 & 02
  • Structured interviews with department heads to document AS-IS processes
  • Gap analysis to identify what needs configuration vs. customisation
  • Finalise Chart of Accounts, cost centres, and workflow approvals
  • Define user roles, permissions matrix, and data security policy
  • Produce a signed Functional Requirement Document (FRD)
Typical duration: 2–3 weeks
⚙️

Configuration & Build

Phase 03
  • Platform installation (cloud or on-premise) and domain setup
  • Module-by-module configuration following the approved FRD
  • Custom print formats — invoices, POs, delivery notes, payslips
  • Third-party integrations: payment gateways, Tally, Shopify, WhatsApp
  • Automated workflows, email alerts, and approval chains configured
Typical duration: 3–5 weeks
📂

Data Migration

Phase 04
  • Extract data from existing systems (Tally, Excel, legacy ERP)
  • Data cleansing — deduplication, standardisation, validation rules
  • Import masters: customers, suppliers, items, employees, assets
  • Opening balances: trial balance, stock quantities, asset register
  • Two-round migration dry runs before final cutover
Typical duration: 2–3 weeks
🎓

Training & UAT

Phase 05
  • Role-specific training sessions: finance, warehouse, HR, sales teams
  • Training documentation and recorded video library handed over
  • User Acceptance Testing (UAT) with real business scenarios
  • Parallel run period — old system and ERP run side-by-side
  • Bug fixes, change requests, and UAT sign-off from stakeholders
Typical duration: 2 weeks
🚀

Go-Live & Hypercare

Phase 06
  • Cutover checklist executed — DNS, backups, access verified
  • Dedicated support engineer on-site or on-call for 2 weeks post-launch
  • Daily check-in calls during hypercare window
  • Performance monitoring, error log review, and fine-tuning
  • Formal handover to Rasy Managed Services team with runbook
Typical duration: 1–2 weeks
🛡️

Ongoing Support & Growth

Post Go-Live
  • Monthly platform updates, security patches applied by Rasy team
  • Helpdesk ticketing with SLA-backed response times
  • Quarterly business reviews and new module advisory
  • Performance tuning, server scaling, and backup management
  • Custom development for new features as the business evolves
Engagement: Ongoing AMC / Managed Service
